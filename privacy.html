Introduction

Crazy Golf VR (“the app,” “we,” “us,” or “our”) is developed and published by Harshvardhan Varma, a solo developer doing business as Fiskar Varmations. This policy applies to all users of Crazy Golf VR. Specific protections that apply to children (users whose Meta account is in the “child” age group, ages 10–12) are described in the Children’s Privacy section below.

Information We Process

We process only the information necessary to operate the app, deliver platform features, and provide support. We do not sell your information. We do not use your information to target advertising. The categories of information we process are:

  • Meta account information. When you sign in through the Meta platform, we receive your Meta (Horizon) user ID, your display name, your profile photo, and your age group (“child” / “teen” / “adult”). We use this to authenticate you, present your identity to other players, and apply age-appropriate feature gates.
  • Meta Avatar. We display your Meta Avatar inside the app using the Meta Avatars SDK. We do not store your avatar customization data — it is fetched from Meta each session and displayed locally and to other players in the same session.
  • Gameplay and progression data. We store your local preferences, achievements, and leaderboard scores so the app can save your progress, present rankings, and prevent cheating. During a multiplayer match, your shot data, position, and game actions are transmitted in real time to the other players in the session.
  • Voice chat (users aged 13 and older only). If you are 13 or older and you enable voice chat, your microphone audio is transmitted in real time to the other players in your session using Vivox, operated by Unity Technologies. Voice audio is not recorded or stored by us. Vivox retains certain non-content metadata for routing and abuse prevention as described in the Voice Chat section.
  • Text chat (where offered, users aged 13 and older only). If text chat is available and you use it, message contents are transmitted via Vivox and may be retained by Vivox for up to seven days for safety and abuse-prevention purposes.
  • Platform social features. When you use follower discovery, party formation, invites, or deep-link “join” features, we process the user IDs and display names of the relevant Meta users (you, your followers, the inviter, and party members) only to enable the requested social action.
  • Blocked users. We receive your Meta block list so we can honor it inside the app — blocked users will not appear in your follower list, invite list, or matchmaking results.
  • Support information. If you contact us at HarshvardhanV98@gmail.com, we process your email address, message contents, and any attachments to respond to your request.
  • Diagnostic information. We collect anonymous crash reports, performance metrics, and error logs through the Meta platform to identify and fix bugs. These reports do not include your voice, text, or chat contents.

Platform Features and Data Use

Crazy Golf VR integrates with the Meta Horizon Platform SDK for the social and platform features below. For each feature we follow Meta’s Developer Data Use Policy and use the data received solely for the purpose stated.

  • User ID and User Profile. We use your Meta user ID and profile (display name + profile photo) to identify you across sessions, display your identity to other players inside the app, and link your progress to your account. We do not use these to identify you across unrelated apps or for advertising.
  • User Age Group. We call Meta’s Get Age Category API to receive a coarse signal (child / teen / adult) for your Meta account. We use this only to apply age-appropriate feature gates inside the app (see Children’s Privacy). We do not receive your date of birth, and we do not use this signal for any other purpose.
  • Avatars. We use the Meta Avatars SDK to display your avatar in the app. Avatar data is fetched from Meta when your session begins and is displayed locally and to other players in the same session. We do not store, repurpose, or transmit avatar data beyond the active session.
  • Followers (sometimes referred to as “Friends”). We use Meta’s Followers API to help you find people you follow on Meta who also play Crazy Golf VR, so you can play together. We process the user IDs and display names of your followers only for the duration of the in-app friends list session and do not retain them afterward.
  • Invites. When you send or receive an invite to a Crazy Golf VR session, we process the user IDs of the sender and recipient and any optional invite message only to deliver the invite. We do not retain invite metadata after the invite is accepted, declined, or expires.
  • Parties. When you join or create a Meta Party that plays Crazy Golf VR together, we process party membership and the user IDs of party members only to provide the party experience — placing your party in the same room and session. We do not use party data outside that experience.
  • Blocked Users. We receive your Meta block list and honor it across the app’s social and matchmaking surfaces. We do not use the block list for any other purpose.
  • Deep Linking (Destinations / Rich Presence). When a friend invites you to a specific session, we use Meta’s deep-link parameters to route you directly to the correct lobby. We process deep-link parameters only at the moment of join.

Voice Chat

Voice chat is available only to users aged 13 and older. If your Meta account is in the “child” age group (ages 10–12), voice chat is disabled for you in Crazy Golf VR and we never request microphone permission — see Children’s Privacy.

When you use voice chat:

  • Your microphone audio is captured by Crazy Golf VR after you grant microphone permission through your Meta Quest device;
  • Audio is transmitted in real time through Vivox infrastructure, operated by Unity Technologies, to the other participants of your voice channel;
  • Voice audio is ephemeral. We do not record, store, transcribe, or moderate voice content with AI. Vivox processes voice as a live transmission and does not retain voice payloads on its servers;
  • Vivox retains certain non-content metadata to operate the service: a truncated IP address, your developer-assigned user ID, and a device identifier, each for up to 30 days, for routing, abuse prevention, and service operations;
  • You can mute yourself, mute other players, or leave a voice channel at any time using the in-app controls or Meta system controls;
  • Other players in your session can hear your voice while you are unmuted. As Meta notes in its platform documentation, voice in a shared virtual space can be heard, copied, or recorded by other users. Please be considerate of what you share.

Unity Technologies acts as our data processor under the GDPR and our service provider under the CCPA for Vivox voice services. Unity’s data-processing terms are available at unity.com/legal/unity-data-processing-addendum-dpa.

Children’s Privacy (Ages 10–12)

Crazy Golf VR is self-certified as a “Mixed Ages” experience on the Meta Horizon Store, meaning it is available to users 10 years of age and older. We believe gaming should be a safe place for kids to play and connect. To protect children’s privacy while keeping the social experience meaningful, we apply the following protections to users whose Meta age category resolves to “child” (ages 10–12):

  • Voice chat is disabled. Child users cannot enable voice chat in Crazy Golf VR, and we never request microphone permission from a child user. Voice audio is never captured.
  • Segregated matchmaking. Child users are matchmade only with other child users in their age range. They do not appear in adult matchmaking pools, and adults do not appear in their matchmaking pools.
  • Non-voice expression. Child users can express themselves through their Meta Avatar’s gestures and facial expressions, emotes, preset phrases (moderated), waves, and in-game celebrations.
  • No advertising identifiers. We do not access or use the Meta Advertising ID for any child user.
  • No behavioral profiling. We do not build behavioral profiles, infer interests, or perform analytics that link to a child user beyond what is necessary to operate gameplay.
  • High privacy by default. All optional privacy-impacting features default to off for child accounts, in alignment with the UK Age-Appropriate Design Code.
  • Limited data collection. The information we process for child users is strictly limited to what is necessary to operate the app: Meta user ID, display name, profile photo, age group, avatar, gameplay state, blocked users, and (within the child pool) followers, parties, and invites.

Parental controls. Meta requires a parent or legal guardian to create and manage the Meta account of any child user aged 10–12, and to verify their identity via approved means such as government ID upload through Yoti or a verified payment method. Meta’s parent-managed account flow includes parental controls for time limits, app installation, social-feature toggles, and account oversight. You can review and adjust those controls through the Meta Family Center at familycenter.meta.com.

COPPA. Meta’s parental consent flow covers the Meta account itself and Meta services — it does not automatically extend to third-party apps such as Crazy Golf VR. Because we do not collect voice from child users, do not use advertising identifiers, do not perform behavioral profiling, and limit data collection to what is strictly necessary to operate the gameplay experience described above, we do not require additional verifiable parental consent under the U.S. Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) for child users of Crazy Golf VR. A parent or legal guardian who believes a child’s information has been collected in error, or who wishes to review or request deletion of a child’s data, may contact us at HarshvardhanV98@gmail.com.

Crazy Golf VR is not designed for, and Meta does not permit, use by children under the age of 10.

Data Storage and Retention

We retain personal information only as long as necessary to provide the service or comply with our legal obligations:

  • Account-linked progress, preferences, and leaderboard scores — retained while you maintain an active Crazy Golf VR profile, or until you request deletion.
  • Voice audio — not retained. Transmitted and discarded in real time.

  • Support correspondence — retained for as long as necessary to resolve your request and meet legal obligations.
  • Backups — automated backups may retain data for an additional 30 days after primary deletion before being overwritten.
